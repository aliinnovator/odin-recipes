Chai(Dodh Pati)

Description

A steaming cup of chai infused with aromatic spices like cardamom and cinnamon, offering a comforting yet spicy kick to awaken your senses.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Black tea leaves,
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom pods

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, add milk and bring it to a simmer.
  2. Add black tea leaves and cardamom.
  3. Let it simmer for a few minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add sugar according to taste.
  5. Continue simmering until desired strength and sweetness are reached.
  6. Strain the chai into cups and serve hot.
