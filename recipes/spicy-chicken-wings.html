Spicy Chicken Wings

Description

Crispy, succulent chicken wings coated in a fiery blend of hot sauce and melted butter. Prepare for a flavor explosion with every bite!

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Hot sauce
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the chicken wings with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Bake them in the oven until crispy.
  3. In a saucepan, melt butter and mix it with hot sauce to your desired spiciness level.
  4. Toss the baked wings in the spicy sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Serve with celery sticks and ranch or blue cheese dressing.
