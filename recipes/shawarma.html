Shawarma

Description

Juicy, seasoned meat wrapped in warm pita bread, bursting with Middle Eastern spices and topped with creamy sauce. It's a spicy sensation you won't want to miss!

Ingredients

  • Chicken or beef strips
  • Shawarma seasoning (cumin, paprika, garlic powder, cinnamon, cayenne pepper)
  • Pita bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Tahini sauce or garlic yogurt sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken or beef strips in shawarma seasoning for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the marinated meat in a skillet until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Warm the pita bread in the oven or on a skillet.
  4. Fill each pita with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and the cooked shawarma meat.
  5. Drizzle with tahini sauce or garlic yogurt sauce.
